Self-Awareness

Oliver Stigley

27 March 2016

Self-awareness is knowing one's internal states, preferences, resources and intuitions, according to Daniel Goleman. It is the key domain to emotional intelligence. "The moment you see a raging river, you are already rising above it." It is a mode that maintains self reflection even in times of emotional turbulence.

Mindfulness is very similar, it is a type of way of paying attention, on purpose, non judgementally. It is almost the same as self-awareness.

I had a brief attempt at a body scan, and found it ok. It isn't really my thing.

I liked the statement that emotions are a physiological response. That approach certainly helps objectify them. Treat them like a ball you are holding, which you can put down.
