Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

I'm experimenting with the pomodoro technique, which I have heard of but not used before. I'm also planning my days quite carefully, so I know what I'm trying to achieve each day. I spare 15minutes each day, planning the next day. This makes it easier for me to muck in without hesitation

What did you find interesting?

I found the concept of making small habits over time to achieve big changes, interesting. In particular the use of existing behaviours to trigger those habits.

The same applies to 'think big' 'start small'. In a sense this is almost agile thinking. Breaking large tasks in to small rolling jobs, to make the process smoother and easier to achieve.
